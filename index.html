About

Nidhin Mahesh is a Senior Software Engineer with deep expertise in distributed systems and blockchain infrastructure. He previously served as Lead Engineer at Jupiter Meta, contributing to the development of a Zero Knowledge proof-based Ethereum L2 protocol for consumer-derived insights on Indian markets.

Before that, he co-led the development of the Rubix Layer 1 Blockchain Protocol — building the payment interface, staking mechanisms, and on-chain wallets that established it as a no-gas-fee, green L1 platform.

Nidhin holds a Bachelor's in Technology from Amrita University, where he led the Google Developer Student Club and FOSS Club, driving open-source contributions across the developer community. His published research, in collaboration with Amrita Wireless Networks and TIFAC-Core in Cyber Security, spans blockchain applications in energy markets, social media DRM, and decentralised mobility.

Interests: Blockchain for data provenance  ·  Service Mesh  ·  Flutter  ·  Edge AI
